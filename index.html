EMI Calculator

Calculate your fixed deposit maturity amount based on principal, interest rate, and tenure.

How to Use Each Calculator

EMI Calculator

You can fill any 3 of the 4 fields, Principal amount, Rate of interest, Time period and EMI. The calculator will show the remaining field, total interest, and total amount payable including a pie chart. Please make sure to select the unit for Time.

RD Calculator

Enter your monthly investment amount, interest rate, and tenure for the RD calculator. You can also choose the compounding frequency (monthly, quarterly, etc.). The calculator will show your total investment, interest earned, and maturity amount including a pie chart. Please make sure to select the unit for Time.

FD Calculator

For FD calculation, input your initial investment amount, the annual interest rate, and the tenure. You can select the compounding period (monthly, quarterly, annually). The calculator will display your maturity amount and the interest earned including a pie chart. Please make sure to select the unit for Time.
